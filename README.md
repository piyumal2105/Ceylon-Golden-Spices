## About
Ceylon Golden Spices is a collection of premium Ceylon spices and handcrafted blends. This repository contains product information, packaging guidelines, marketing copy, and recipes for the brand.

## Products
Core product categories:
- Whole spices: cinnamon quills, black peppercorns, cloves
- Ground spices: cinnamon powder, turmeric, curry powders
- Signature blends: garam masala, roasted curry, chai blend
- Speciality items: spice kits, gift boxes

Each product entry should include:
- Name, SKU
- Net weight and serving size
- Ingredients and allergen info
- Suggested uses and shelf life

## Quality & sourcing
- Single-origin Ceylon sourcing
- Small-batch processing, minimal preservatives
- Traceability records for farm and harvest when available
- Basic quality checks: moisture, aroma, contamination screening
